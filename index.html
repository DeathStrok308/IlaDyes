About Us

Incorporated in 2019, Our company focuses on eco friendly dyeing process. Over years, humans have polluted air, water and land and this is our step to bring in sustainablitity in world and reduce pollution, and wastage. We use a chemical free method which uses recycled water and the water can also be used to other purposes like watering plants. The colours are derived from vegeta- bles, fruits, seeds, flowers and leaves. Also, the fabrics and garments dyed from our dye are safe to your body. It does not cause any infections to your body. We are certified natu- ral dyers.
